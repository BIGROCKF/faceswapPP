Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

User-Provided Content

Our App allows users to upload images and videos for AI-based face processing and content generation. Uploaded content is used only to provide the requested features.

Face Data

The App temporarily processes facial features detected from user-submitted images or videos in order to perform AI-based visual transformations. No biometric templates, facial recognition profiles, or permanent identifiers are created or stored.

Account Information

When you sign in using a third-party service such as Google, we may receive basic account information such as your email address and a unique user ID.

Purchase Information

The App offers subscription plans and credit-based purchases. All payments are securely processed by the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment or credit card information.

2. How We Use Your Information
  • To provide AI-based image and video processing features
  • To process face data and generate requested results
  • To manage subscriptions and credit usage
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To respond to user support requests

3. Face Data Processing and Storage

Face data is processed exclusively for providing AI-based transformations requested by the user. Processing is performed using secure AI services.

Face data is not used for identification, tracking, biometric profiling, advertising, or analytics purposes.

User-submitted images, videos, and associated face data are processed temporarily and are automatically deleted after processing is completed and results are delivered to the user.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. Face data is not shared with third parties for marketing, advertising, or tracking purposes. Data is only processed through secure AI infrastructure required to operate core features.

5. Data Retention

User-generated content and face data are retained only for the duration necessary to complete processing. Once processing is completed, all related data is automatically deleted.

Account-related information is retained until the user deletes their account.

6. Account Deletion

Users may delete their account at any time within the App. When an account is deleted, all associated personal data and content are permanently removed from our systems.
